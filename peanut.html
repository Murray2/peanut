Peanut Butter Truffles

Recipe:

  • 4 oz milk chocolate
  • 1 lb natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 12 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 12 tbsp unsalted butter
    •
  • 1 lb bittersweet chocolate
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    •

Procedure:

Place salt and sugar in a bowl.


Melt chocolate in the microwave, stir every 10-15 sec. Be careful not to over cook your chocolate or it will become grainy. So stop when there are small lumps and stir them until they dissolve.

Warm peanut butter in the microwave gently. Mix together with the sugar and sea salt.

Add the melted chocolate and then the room temperature butter. Mix together until well combined.

Refrigerate mixture. When cooled, shape into balls quickly and put them back into the fridge. When they are cool, melt your second amount of chocolate in the microwave and dip in the chocolate (you can dip them with chopsticks or a folk).

Refrigerate until the chocolate hardens and dust your chocolates with cocoa powder.

ENJOY!
